Rubber Band Trick

The Jumping Rubber Band

You show a single rubber band around your index and middle finger. You then make a magical gesture and make it jump to your ring and pinky finger!

Rubber band magic is great because you can always have a rubber band around your wrist.

  1. Put the Rubber Band on Two Fingers
    Stretch the rubber band around your index finger and middle finger.

  2. Secret Setup
    While the rubber band is stretched on those two fingers: Use your other hand to pull the rubber band outward. Quietly put all four fingers (index, middle, ring, and pinky) inside the rubber band. From the front, it should still look like the band is only on two fingers.

  3. Close Your Hand
    Close your hand into a loose fist so no one sees the setup.

  4. The Magic Moment
    Open your hand quickly. The rubber band will jump from your index & middle fingers to your ring & pinky fingers! It looks like the rubber band magically teleported.

  5. Do It Again (optional)
    You can secretly reset it and make it jump back to the first two fingers.
