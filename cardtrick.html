The cross cut force

A spectator cuts the deck freely and you know what card they cut to every time

This is a very reliable card force that requires no slight of hand at all

  1. Know the top card
    Secretly look at the top card of the deck. This is the card you will force.

  2. Have the spectator cut the deck
    Ask the spectator to cut the deck anywhere they like.

  3. Mark the cut (important!)
    Take the top half of the deck and place it crosswise on top of the bottom half (at a 90Â° angle).

  4. Pause and misdirect
    Talk for a few seconds.

  5. Reveal the forced card
    Lift off the crosswise packet and ask the spectator to look at the top card of the remaining pile. That card is the one you peeked at in Step 1.
